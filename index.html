Welcome to GnomGnom's Utils.

GnomGnom's Utils is a mod for Minecraft that adds a bunch of useful stuff to the game, some of which are new, others are from dead mods that arnt coming back.

Content

Currently we have in the game

  • Sortivator Block. -- sorts the inventory of the ajasent chest it is pointing at, like inventory tweaks, but its a block that does it for you when receaving a redstone signal.
    • Creeper Mite -- not a utility, but i can think of some arguments as to why it is. its a new mob in minecraft that lives in long grass, there is a small chance that when breaking crass looking for seeds or whatnot, you may come across one, they are not dangerous on their own, but they will call friends from nearby to help take you out.
    • Damara's Remedy -- a block that can grow any crop, including cactus or sugercane without having water nearby, it also has the added effect of when they are stacked ontop of each other, they can speed the growth of the crop.
